Joseph Cappelli is a NYC based actor who has appeared in many Off-Broadway and regional productions (Ghost of War at Theatre Row Studios, Paradise, Comedy of Errors), television( I Married a Mobster ) and commercials (FourSquare).

He is continually studying the Eric Morris System of acting with the great Anthony Vincent Bova, and is focusing on transitioning into comedy, voice-over, and commercial work. Joe is also in the midst of writing an original comedic web series, which he is looking to produce by the end of 2014.

Joe suggests you head on over to his vine account to view a couple of belly laugh inducing videos (one of which was featured on the Huffington Post).
